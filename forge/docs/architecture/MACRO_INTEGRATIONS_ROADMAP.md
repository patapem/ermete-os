# Ermete OS Macro Integrations Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the 8 Big Tech integrations planned for Ermete OS:

## Core Components
1. **ermete-updater-rs**: OTA & Delta Updates
2. **ermete-telemetry-rs**: Crash Reporting
3. **ermete-store-rs**: App Store & Sandboxing
4. **ermete-cloud-rs**: Continuity & Sync
5. **ermete-mdm-rs**: Find My Device & MDM
6. **xdg-desktop-portal-ermete**: Privacy Portals

## Security & Firmware
7. **Secure Boot Measured**
8. **LVFS Fwupd**
